  • Why WeAmity?

A platform for collaborating students from every Amity University Campus with product/service based startups in order to provide the students an opportunity to resume worthy Projects/Internships, Exposure and Earn through part-time contribution while gaining a hands-on experience in the field of Technology, Management, Arts and Law.

Only those product/service based startups can register for collaboration which fullfills the following criteria :-

  • One of the Director of startup should be a student or alumni of Amity University.
  • Total annual turn over of the startup should be less than 1 Crore Indian Rupees in the last financial year.
  • Should be an incorporated startup with a registered Sole Proprietorship, Partnership Firm or Private Limited Company.
  • Should produce document of incorporation while registration.
